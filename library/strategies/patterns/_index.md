# Patterns

Good moves that apply across pivots and phases. Where `pivots/` are specific edges, patterns are *habits of mind* you carry everywhere.

## Children
- **[[pattern-selector-discipline]]** — the selector log: every value carries type, provenance, and confidence, so the case is auditable, reversible, and handoff-safe. The backbone discipline.
- **[[pattern-breadth-before-depth]]** — run every cheap pivot off everything you hold before deepening any one lead; the structural defense against tunnel vision.
- **[[pattern-reverse-image-everything]]** — reflexively reverse-search every image before reasoning about it; the cheapest high-yield move there is.
- **[[pattern-network-triangulation]]** — a private subject is visible through their network; reframe "what did they post?" as "who's around them?". (Doctrine for the pivot of the same name.)
- **[[pattern-timeline-anchoring]]** — order every dated artifact into a last-known-activity timeline; surfaces the freshest signal and exposes contradictions.

## The through-line
Two of these (selector-discipline, breadth-before-depth) are about *how you operate*; the other three are *reflexes* you fire on contact with an image, a network, or a set of dates. Together they're most of what separates a disciplined investigation from a lucky one.
