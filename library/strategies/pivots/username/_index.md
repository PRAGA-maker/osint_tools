# Pivots from `username`

People reuse handles across platforms out of convenience, so one known username often unlocks a whole footprint. This is typically the highest-yield early pivot in a handle-seeded case.

## Sub-pivots
- **`social-profile/`** → [[username-reuse-enumeration]] — enumerate the handle (and variants) across platforms, confirm each hit, and harvest the selectors every confirmed profile leaks.
