# Pivots from `phone`

A phone number is close to one person. The defining tension is passive vs active: database lookups are contactless, but messaging-app checks and any direct contact can tip off the subject.

## Sub-pivots
- **`name/`** → [[pivot-phone-to-identity]] — caller-ID/spam databases, people-search, and (carefully) messaging-app presence to reach a name, photo, and accounts.
